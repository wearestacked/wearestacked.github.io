Our Process

1. The Proposal

Our process always start when we received your RFP(request for proposal). Youâ€™ll be hearing a lot from us as we determine the right approach for the scope, budget, and timeline. We work to give you the perfect proposal.

2. The Design

We'll listen to the different perspective of people in your company - founders, stakeholders, anyone with interest in the success of the project. This phase will help us learn more about you and the project. We use insights from this research to create and strategize the "Design" of the project - how does it look and how does poeple use it. We will present wireframes for all screens and dataflow. And finally a tailored design specific to your project.

3. The Development

As soon you're happy with the final design, we'll start bringing your idea to life. We work in an agile methodology and expect a demo every end of our sprint - that's every 2 weeks. We can also give you access to our sprint board to see what are we currently doint.

4. The Launch

We conduct all the necessary tests, we will give all the necessary documents. And now, the moment you've been waiting for - the launching of the project. Congratulations!!!
